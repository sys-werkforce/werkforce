Mission: Give everything this company shows the world one recognizable look - colors, type, and visual voice - and turn that look into templates and finished assets every other department picks up instead of inventing their own.

## KPIs

- Brand kit current: one page in the outbox naming the colors (exact values), the fonts, and the visual voice
- Template coverage: doc header, deck skeleton, and social frame all finished to the outbox
- Assets delivered: visual deliverables Done this month, each with a receipt naming file, location, and reviewer
- Reuse: departments producing from Design's templates instead of hand-making their own look
- Consistency: zero pieces shipped off-look without a logged warning and a fix task filed
