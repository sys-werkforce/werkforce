# Strategy - five starter task ideas

Pick 1-3 your business actually needs this week; each becomes a Filed board row.

- Draft the watchlist: the 3-5 competitors or alternatives our ideal customer actually compares us to, one line each on why they matter, from `company/business-model.md`
- Run the first teardown dossier on the closest competitor: what they sell, price, angle, what to steal and what to avoid - every claim with a source and date
- Draft a one-page pricing map of the field: names, prices, and what each price includes, from cited pages only - blanks stay `(not set yet)` where nothing verifiable exists
- Land the first cited pattern in `company/playbooks.md` from the first reviewed dossier - Pattern, Evidence, Fails when, Fits when
- Draft the "how we are different" one-pager for the founder: our angle versus the field's, built from dossier evidence (a draft only - public claims are a reserved decision)
