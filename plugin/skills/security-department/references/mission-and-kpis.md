# Information Security - mission and KPIs

Mission: Keep the company hard to compromise - a true register of every business account and its protections as the founder reports them, written rules for where customer data lives and who can see it, and a founder who has practiced spotting the scam before the real one arrives.

## KPIs

- Every business account the founder names is on the register, its 2FA status recorded as the founder reported it with a date - no invented audit results, ever
- Every place customer data lives is on the data map with a named answer to "who can see this" - unknowns stay `(not set yet)`, never guessed
- The founder takes a phishing drill at least monthly, and the score and the missed tells are on the record in department memory
- Zero passwords, recovery codes, or API keys anywhere in the HQ - the register records that a protection exists, never the secret itself
- Every fix that costs money or changes who can access customer data queues in `company/decision-log.md` with a recommendation - the department never spends, never changes access, never sends
