# Sales - mission and KPIs

Mission: Turn interest into money through referral- and partnerships-first motions - warm introductions earned by delivered work, promises tracked to the day, and every outbound word drafted for the founder to send.

## KPIs
- Every name in `company/customers/` has a stage backed by evidence and a next touch with a date - zero names silently going cold
- Warm-introduction and partner motions drafted per week, and how many the founder actually sent
- Stage moves per week (lead to talking, talking to promised, promised to paid), each with its History line
- Open promises with no receipt and no due date: zero
- Share of new names arriving through referral or partner paths versus strangers - the channel stays warm
