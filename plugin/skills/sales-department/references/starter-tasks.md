# Sales - starter task ideas

- Run the first pipeline pass: walk every name in `company/customers/`, confirm each stage against its evidence, set a next touch with a date, and note the three most overdue names for the board
- Draft a warm-introduction ask to your happiest customer - who do they know who has the same problem you just solved (draft to the outbox; the founder sends)
- Build the partner shortlist: ten people or companies who already serve your ideal customer, one line each on why a genuine introduction path exists
- Draft the referral thank-you for the last person who sent someone your way, with one specific line about what happened to their introduction (draft only; sending is reserved)
- Write the qualification one-pager: the five questions every new lead gets asked before any proposal or price is discussed, in plain language
