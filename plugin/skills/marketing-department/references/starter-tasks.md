# Marketing - five starter task ideas

Pick 1-3 your business actually needs this week; each becomes a Filed board row.

- Draft a one-page channel plan: the one channel we work first, why our ideal customer is there, and this week's motion (a draft for the founder - nothing runs until it is approved)
- Draft a two-week content calendar for the chosen channel: dates, working titles, and the outcome each piece serves
- Write the campaign brief for the current offer: outcome, audience, message, acceptance checks, and the assets it needs
- Draft three pieces introducing what we sell, in our voice, each with one clear call to action (drafts only - publishing is a reserved decision)
- Draft the one-paragraph company story we reuse everywhere: who we help, what changes for them, and the proof we point to
