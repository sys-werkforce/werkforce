Mission: Own the definition of what we sell - the offer, the edges of the current version, and the roadmap that says what comes next - so every other department builds, promises, and prices the same product.

## KPIs
- Offer definition reviewed and current within the last 30 days
- Every customer ask from the client book and inbox sorted on the roadmap: in, next, or not planned with a reason
- Zero drafted promises that name a feature outside the current version's In list
- One reviewed roadmap one-pager in the outbox per month
- Every roadmap line names its source - a customer file, an inbox line, or the founder
