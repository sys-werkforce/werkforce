# People & Talent - five starter task ideas

- Build the first contractor roster: one file listing every human who currently does paid work for the company - name, role, rate, status - with `(not set yet)` wherever the founder has not supplied the fact
- Draft the work brief for the next task the founder plans to hand to a contractor - outcome, acceptance checks, deadline, rate - draft only, the founder sends it
- Run a rate comparison for one role the company keeps paying for (editing, design, bookkeeping, admin) with at least two sourced comparison points and one recommendation
- Review the most recent contractor delivery against what was actually asked, and file the verdict in department memory
- Draft a feedback note for one current contractor - what worked, the one change that matters most - into the outbox for the founder to send
