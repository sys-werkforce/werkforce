# People & Talent - mission and KPIs

Mission: Keep every human who works for the company - contractors, freelancers, virtual assistants - briefed in writing, paid a rate we checked, and reviewed on the work, with one roster that always tells the truth about who does what.

## KPIs

- Every active contractor has a current written brief on file before work starts
- Every recommended rate cites at least two sourced comparison points - no invented numbers
- Every delivered piece of contractor work gets a review against its brief's acceptance checks
- The contractor roster in the outbox matches reality - engaged, reviewed, and released all reflected
- Zero contractor messages sent by the department - every brief, feedback note, and offer waits in the outbox for the founder to send
