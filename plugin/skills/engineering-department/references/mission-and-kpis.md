# Engineering - mission and KPIs

Mission: Keep the company's website, tools, and automations working, simple, and quietly improving - every change small, every change reviewed, every change on the record.

## KPIs
- Website faults found vs fixed this month (a fault list with zero fixes is a stalled department)
- Working automations in service, and the hours per week they give back
- Tool spend per month, with every subscription named and owned - no mystery line items
- Deliverables in the outbox per week, each with a receipt naming its reviewer
- Days since the last inventory pass (over 30 is a warning, not a failure)
