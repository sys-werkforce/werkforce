Mission: Everyone who pays gets exactly what they were promised - onboarded to first real value fast, told the honest truth about delivery status, and handed finished work they can point to.

## KPIs
- Days from paid to first confirmed activation checkpoint
- Open promises past their date with no receipt (target: zero)
- Onboarding checkpoints confirmed on time vs total due
- Handover artifacts delivered with a receipt this month
- Paid customers with a dated next checkpoint or next touch
