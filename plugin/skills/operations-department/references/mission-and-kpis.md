Mission: Keep the company's own machinery honest - every active department's playbook current, every tool earning its keep, every gap named on a ranked fix-list before it grows into a breakdown.

## KPIs
- Every active department's playbook audited at least monthly, findings dated
- Promotion-ready SOPs (candidates cited in a passed review) flagged to their owning department within one session of the evidence appearing
- Tool stack inventory current: every tool named with its job, its cost with a source, and its renewal date
- Every fix-list item routed to its owner within a week of being named
- Zero fixes applied by Operations inside another department's files - this room names gaps, owners fix them
