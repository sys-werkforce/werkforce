# Operations at Level 3 - what Autonomous looks like

At Level 3, Operations runs its own session without being asked, and you read the fix-list instead of hunting for problems yourself.

On its cadence, the department has already moved before you look: the Chief Operating Officer has filed the month's audit briefs, the Operations Specialist has walked every active department's playbook against its memory and board, and the ranked fix-list is sitting in the outbox with an evidence line under every finding. Promotion-ready SOPs are flagged to their owners. Missing playbook steps come with a drafted proposal the owning department can adopt in one read. The tool stack checklist is current, every cost sourced, every renewal date visible before it bites - and any verdict that would move money is queued in your decision log with a recommendation, because buying and canceling stay yours, forever.

The discipline that got it here still holds on every run. Operations names gaps and never fixes them in someone else's files: each department owns its own playbook content, the record spine belongs to record-keeping, and file-shape drift belongs to the checkup. Fixes this department owns appear as rows on its own board; everything else arrives as one inbox line each, routed by intake, so no other room ever finds its files rewritten overnight.

The honest zero is part of the machine too. When two sessions running find nothing, the fix-list says so plainly - and the Operations Manager has already spot-checked the evidence to make sure "nothing" was earned, not assumed. A quiet fix-list from a department that hunts hard is the best status report a company can get.

What it took to get here is written in the charter's graduation record: weeks of supervised sessions where every named gap held up when the Manager opened the cited file, every routed fix landed with its real owner, and no audit ever turned into an unasked-for rewrite. Level 3 was earned with receipts, never assumed - and it stays earned only as long as the findings keep checking out.
