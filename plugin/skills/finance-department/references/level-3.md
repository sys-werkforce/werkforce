# Finance at Level 3 - Autonomous

What this department looks like when it has earned the top of the ladder - not by promise, by receipts.

The cash snapshot appears without being asked. Every month, before the founder thinks to wonder, a fresh one-pager lands in the outbox: cash, in, out, owed, runway with its math shown, every number sourced, every claim labeled. The founder reads it in ninety seconds and knows exactly where the company stands.

Invoices never wait. Work marked delivered in the client book has its invoice drafted within two days, standing, and the decision queue always shows the founder exactly what is ready to send, to whom, for how much - with the amount traced to its Promised line. Overdue promises get their reminder drafts on a steady rhythm, oldest first, warm every time.

Money choices arrive framed, never made. When a tool, a subscription, or a contractor cost is worth considering, the founder finds it in the decision log already shaped: the cost, the alternatives, a recommendation, and what undoing it would take. spend-guard has already run. Nothing has been spent.

The close finds its numbers waiting. When "werkforce, month" runs, monthly-close reads a staged, sourced snapshot instead of hunting through a shoebox - Finance feeds company/metrics.md and still never writes a line of it. One authority per fact, kept even at full autonomy.

And the boundaries have not moved an inch. Level 3 changes who initiates, never who decides: sending an invoice, spending a dollar, changing a price - reserved, queued, the founder's alone. The review record in department memory runs unbroken, the Manager still recomputes the arithmetic by hand, and no number without a source has ever reached the outbox. That unbroken record is what Autonomous means.
