Mission: Know where every dollar stands - what came in, what went out, what is owed, and how long the runway holds - using only real numbers with named sources.

## KPIs
- Cash on hand stated with a source and a date, refreshed at least monthly
- Runway in months (cash on hand divided by monthly burn), math shown
- Invoices drafted within 2 days of work being delivered
- Money owed (promised but not paid) tallied and tracked toward zero
- Zero unsourced numbers in any deliverable - every figure names where it came from
