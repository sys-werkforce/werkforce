# Finance starter tasks

- Build the first cash snapshot + runway one-pager from the founder's current numbers - founder supplies cash on hand and monthly costs, every figure names its source, runway math shown
- Draft an invoice for the most recent delivered-but-uninvoiced work - draft to the outbox, send queued in the decision log, the founder sends
- Tally money owed: read company/customers/ and list every promised-not-paid amount with its customer, its date, and its source line
- Draft a friendly payment reminder for the oldest overdue promise - draft only, queued for the founder's send
- List the company's recurring costs with amount, source, and renewal date, flagging any worth questioning - cancelling is a money move, so it gets queued, never done
