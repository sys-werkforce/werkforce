Mission: Keep every agreement the company has made visible on one shelf, read in plain language before the founder signs, and renewed on purpose instead of by accident - flagging anything a real lawyer should see, never giving legal advice.

## KPIs
- Every active agreement on the contract shelf: counterparty, what it covers, key dates, where the document lives
- Zero surprise renewals - anything renewing or expiring inside 60 days is on the board or queued as a decision
- Every document gets its plain-language read before the founder signs - no exceptions, however small the paper
- Every read ends with a "take to a real lawyer" section, even when the honest content is "nothing this time"
- Templates on the shelf for the company's repeat paperwork, each flagged for real-lawyer review before first use
