# Legal starter tasks

- Build the first contract shelf: list every agreement the company has made - client contracts, tool subscriptions, leases, anything signed - with counterparty, key dates, renewal terms, and where each document lives, every line naming its source
- Run the renewal radar on the new shelf: flag everything renewing, expiring, or hitting a notice window in the next 90 days, and queue anything that needs a founder decision with a recommendation attached
- Plain-language read of the next document waiting on a signature (or the most recently signed one, as practice): what it says, what is unusual, what to ask about, and the take-to-a-real-lawyer flags
- Draft the company's most-repeated agreement as a template for the shelf - draft only, flagged for real-lawyer review before first use, and sending it to anyone is queued for the founder, never sent
- Build the "ask a real lawyer" one-pager: collect every open flag and unanswered question from the reads into one page the founder can take into a single efficient conversation with counsel
